About Me

Hi! My name is Jodi and I am a web developer.
Before now, you could find me climbing mountains in Alaska and swimming in the waters of Hawaii. I grew up in a small town in New Mexico, climbing hay bails and playing hide and seek in corn fields, spending our summers in Mexico at the property my family owned and used to build homes for families and orphans; some of my favorite memories. Once I grew up, I was determined to see more of the world. And that, I did. I traveled to five countries with a missions orgonization after high school; which only planted the travel bug even deeper. Taking me to a few more countires and moving to different states.
I put off pursuing a career for the reason of not fully knowing what I really wanted to dive head first into. And then, there was a web development bootcamp. Barely any questions asked, I knew I needed to learn and pursue this. I have always liked creating easy template websites for friend's school portfolios/resumes or family's businesses, I thought, 'why would I not pursue the real deal?' Template websites are only good for sparing you a headache. But with headache and all, learning to code has been an extremely exciting journey that I only get more excited about. As I find myself thinking about it while I'm doing other things (that's when you know you've found your thing).
I have now settled down a bit, and am living in Fort Collins, Colorado with my fiancé and our alaskan malamute, Wazza. We still both enjoy traveling when we can, but are eager to start our careers and life together.
In my free time you can find me doing anything creative, enjoying outside, or taking photos of everything. I love the sun and snow just the same. And I really love people.
Thanks for reading ☺
